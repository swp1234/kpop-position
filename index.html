    About the K-POP Position Test

    How the K-POP Position Test Works

    The K-POP Position Test asks 12 fun questions about your personality, social behavior, and creative preferences. Based on your answers, it analyzes which of the 7 classic K-POP idol positions best matches you — from Main Vocal and Lead Dancer to Rapper, Visual, Leader, Maknae, and All-Rounder. You also get matched to real K-POP idols who share your position.

    What Your K-POP Position Says About You

    Your K-POP position reveals your natural role in group settings. Main Vocals are confident performers, Lead Dancers are dedicated perfectionists, Rappers are creative expressionists, Visuals are charming communicators, and Leaders are responsible coordinators. Understanding your position can give fun insights into your real-life strengths.
